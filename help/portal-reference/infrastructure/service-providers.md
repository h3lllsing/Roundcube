## Overview

The Service Providers module stores details about external companies and vendors you contract with. Providers can be linked to VPS, VoIP, Domains, Hostings, and other infrastructure modules.

## Permission Controls

| Control | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Access | View and list service providers |
| Manage | Create, edit, and delete providers |

## Features

### Provider Details
- Company name, website, and contact information
- Support portal URLs and account numbers
- Billing cycle and cost tracking

### Cross-Module Linking
Providers are referenced by VPS, VoIP, Domain, Hosting, Domain Email, and Other Service records, giving a consolidated view of vendor relationships.
