# Quick Start Guide

> **Audience:** All Users — **Purpose:** Complete your first login and learn how to navigate the portal

## Logging In

Access the portal at your OpsPilot URL. Enter your email and password to log in.

## Dashboard

After logging in, the Dashboard shows an overview of your key metrics, upcoming renewals, and recent activity.

## Finding Records Across Modules

Use the Global Search bar in the top navigation to search across all modules.

## Updating Your Profile

Click your avatar in the top-right corner and select My Profile to update your name, email, and password.
