## General

### What is the OpsPilot portal?
OpsPilot is a centralised operations management portal for tracking infrastructure (servers, domains, hostings), credentials, tasks, and team access.

### How do I reset my password?
Go to the Login screen and click **Forgot Password?**. Enter your email address and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### I can't log in. What should I do?
1. Check that Caps Lock is off
2. Use the **Forgot Password?** link to reset
3. If still blocked, contact your administrator — your account may be suspended

## Permissions

### Why can't I see a module in the sidebar?
Either the module is disabled by a Super Admin, or your roles don't include **Access** for that module. Check [My Permissions](/my-permissions) to see what you have access to.

### What does "Reveal" mean?
Reveal is a permission control that grants the ability to view decrypted credential values (passwords, API keys). See [Credential Reveal](/credential-reveal).

### How do I get Full Access?
Full Access is a role assignment. Ask your administrator to assign or create a role with Full Access enabled.

## Records

### I deleted a record by mistake. Can I recover it?
Records are soft-deleted and can be restored by a Super Admin within the retention period. Contact your administrator.

### How do I export my data?
Use the Export feature from the module's listing page. You need the Export privilege. See [Import / Export](/help/portal-reference/administrator/import-export).

### What file formats are supported for import?
CSV files only. Download the template from the Import screen for the correct column format.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest two major versions).

### The portal seems slow. What can I check?
- Your internet connection
- Try clearing your browser cache
- Check if other users are experiencing the same issue

### I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please report issues through your organisation's support channel. Include:
- What you were doing
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- Browser and operating system
