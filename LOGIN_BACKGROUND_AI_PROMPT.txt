MAIN PROMPT
================================================================

A premium enterprise login page background illustration for
OpsPilot, an Enterprise IT Operations Platform. 16:9 aspect
ratio, ultra-detailed 4K resolution.

The artwork uses THREE EXPLICIT DEPTH LAYERS to create spatial
depth through opacity, blur, scale, and shadow — NOT through 3D
rendering. All shapes use rounded corners (minimum 8px). All lines
are thin (1.5-2px) and semi-transparent. All fills are low-opacity
indigo-to-purple gradients (6-15%).


BACKGROUND LAYER — lowest opacity, largest scale, Gaussian blur 40-80px:

- Repeating 40x40px engineering grid pattern — indigo (#6366f1)
  at 3% opacity across the entire canvas.
- Soft radial ambient glow in indigo (#eef2ff/60%) from center-left,
  fading to transparent at edges.
- 3-4 large rounded geometric blobs in deep indigo (#4f46e5) at
  3-5% opacity, deeply blurred (60px), scattered behind the composition.


MIDDLE LAYER — moderate opacity (8-15%), standard scale, no blur:

- OPERATIONS CORE: A large frosted glass hub at the exact center
  of the composition. 4 soft elliptical concentric rings in indigo
  and purple at 6-12% opacity with a radial glow center. Glassmorphism
  effect: semi-transparent white fill (rgba(255,255,255,0.30)) with
  40px backdrop blur, subtle border, soft glow shadow. The rings are
  slightly off-center from each other to suggest dynamic processing.
  This is the heart of the platform — all other objects connect to it.

- INTERNET: Wireframe globe hemisphere near upper-left. 3-4 thin
  concentric arcs radiate outward. Small glowing center node.
  Stroke: indigo (#6366f1) at 15% opacity, 1.5px.

- CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE: Two clusters of 5-6 overlapping rounded
  rectangles in a cloud formation, center-upper area. Gradient fill:
  deep indigo (#4f46e5) at 10% fading to purple (#9333ea) at 6%.
  Stroke at 20% opacity.

- DNS: Three concentric arc rings with dotted connection lines
  descending downward. Upper-right. Stroke: indigo (#6366f1) at
  12% opacity, dotted. One accent ring in light purple (#a855f7)
  at 8%.

- DOMAINS: A loose grid of 6 small rounded rectangles (3 rows by
  2 columns) directly below the DNS rings. Fill: indigo (#6366f1)
  at 10%. Stroke at 20%, rounded corners 6px.

- HOSTING: Three wide horizontal rounded panels stacked vertically,
  left-center area. Fill: deep indigo (#4f46e5) at 8%. Stroke at 18%.

- VPS: A larger rounded rectangle subdivided internally by thin
  vertical lines into 4 partitions, positioned above the server rack.
  Outer fill: light purple (#a855f7) at 8%. Dividers at 15%.

ALL middle-layer objects have subtle drop shadows (black at 3%,
4px blur) and connect to the Operations Core via network lines.


FOREGROUND LAYER — highest opacity (12-40%), sharpest edges,
noticeable shadows (black at 5%, 8px blur):

- SERVER RACK: The PRIMARY VISUAL ANCHOR — positioned center, directly
  IN FRONT of the Operations Core. Three stacked horizontal server
  modules in isometric or flat-layered style. Each module has 2 small
  LED indicator dots (one indigo, one emerald) and 4-5 horizontal
  ventilation slit lines. Gradient fill: deep indigo (#4f46e5) at
  12% fading to purple (#9333ea) at 8%. Stroke: indigo (#6366f1) at
  25%, 2px, rounded corners 12px. ~350x500px.

- NETWORK LINES: Sweeping cubic bezier curves radiating FROM the
  Operations Core outward to every object in the composition. Lines
  are thicker (2px) near the Core and taper to thinner (1px) at
  endpoints, suggesting energy disbursement. Stroke: indigo (#6366f1)
  at 15-25%, 1.5px. Small glowing data nodes (6-8px circles) at
  key intersections.

- ASSET MANAGEMENT: A tidy 3x3 grid of tiny rounded squares in the
  lower-left corner. Fill: indigo (#6366f1) at 8-10%. 2-3 squares
  have small colored indicator dots (emerald or amber).

- MONITORING DASHBOARD: Abstract chart cluster. One smooth rising
  curve line in indigo (#6366f1) at 30% with a subtle gradient fill
  area below. A single 6px emerald (#10b981) dot at the curve peak
  (success indicator). Four vertical bars of varying height in light
  purple (#a855f7) at 12-18%. One donut arc segment. No axes, no
  labels, no gridlines.

- EMAIL: A small geometric envelope-like shape with a triangular
  fold detail, positioned to the right of the server rack.
  Fill: indigo (#6366f1) at 10%. Stroke at 20%.

- ANALYTICS: Three varied geometric shapes — a diamond, a triangle,
  and a hexagon — connected by thin lines. All have rounded corners.
  Fill: light purple (#a855f7) at 10-15%. Lines at 12%.

- NOTIFICATION FLOW: A gently curved dotted path from the server rack
  toward the right edge. 5-8 small streaming circles (4-6px)
  distributed along the path in indigo (#6366f1) at 40%. One dot in
  amber (#f59e0b) at 30% as a warning indicator.

- SECURITY SHIELD: A large semi-transparent shield outline on the far
  right edge of the illustration zone, acting as a transition toward
  the empty safe zone. Stroke: indigo (#6366f1) at 20-30%, 2.5px.
  No fill. Subtle glow behind the shield: radial indigo at 8%,
  60px blur.
